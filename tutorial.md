WELCOME TO HYPERSPACE

Here's your cursor: 

CRANK to steer, UP to accelerate, RIGHT/A to follow [link](https://orbit.casa/back.md), DOWN/LEFT to scroll a full page.

Checkout the websites in the [directory](https://orbit.casa/directory.md).

Open the menu to save the current page; closing the menu loads the selected "open" page. You can also add saved pages by editing the game file using your computer (visit https://orbit.casa on your computer to see how to do that).

ORBIT is very young (born on 2025.11.13) and lots of things can be improved - check out the code repo at https://orbit.casa (on your computer/phone) to see how you can contribute!
